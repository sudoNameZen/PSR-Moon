IDEA OF OUR MODEL :

● Infrared waves are used in many Research papers for low light enhancement. We incorporated similar approach to enhance the PSR.
● In order to enhance the PSR of the moon, we are combining the two datasets of different spectrum, i.e., OHRC (Visible) & IIRS (Infrared).
● We acquired data from PRADAN platform and processed it in QGIS & python.
● Further, images are given to two separate Encoders which generates two different embeddings for each image.
● Structural Embeddings (SE) for IIRS image , stores information about the structure visible in the IIRS dataset.
● Resolution Embeddings (RE) for storing information regarding the resolution of OHRC image.
● These embeddings are given to a Decoder which combines the information of both the embeddings and creates a new image using GAN deep learning model.
● Enhanced image is sent back to the encoder which generates the embeddings (SE & RE) which is compared with the embeddings of the original images .
● Loss is calculated between both the embeddings of original and enhanced images .
● With the help of gradient descent, improved embeddings are sent back to GAN.
● Process continued until optimized results are obtained.
